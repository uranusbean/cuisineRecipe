Ingredient
Pairing
There are at least 200,000 edible ingredients and more than 40 cuisines in the world. Based upon regions, cuisines may be devided to African cuisines, cuisines of the Americas, Asian cuisines, and European cuisines. This food ingredient pairing visualization will help you understand the following questions:
What are the food ingredient combinations in each cuisine?
Which ingredient(s) are more popular?
