Food Pairing
Visualization of Cuisine and Ingredient
There are at least 200,000 edible ingredients and more than 40 cuisines in the world. Based upon regions, cuisines may be devided to Asian cuisines, European cuisines, cuisines of the Americas and African cuisines.

In this project, we try to compare and contrast the usage of food ingredients in different cuisines. We are also interested in exploring the most popular combinations among ingredients in one particular cuisine. Since we hypothesize is that the cuisine similarity is associated with region and/ or country, we also want to examine our hypothesis and to explore how similar between two cuisines.
